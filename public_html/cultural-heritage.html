Cultural Heritage

Throughout 2025, I photographed over 4,000 objects across Portuguese museums as part of a project sponsored by Património Cultural IP. The initiative focused on digitizing collections from key institutions in central Portugal, like Museu José Malhoa and Museu de Cerâmica (Caldas da Rainha), Mosteiro de Santa Clara-a-Velha (Coimbra), and Museu José Manso (Nazaré). The collections featured a wide variety of object types, including paintings and drawings to sculptures, archaeological artifacts, and ceramics.
