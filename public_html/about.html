Photographer, journalist


Born and raised in São Paulo, Brazil, I've been through this journey in photography and journalism for about 20 years.

For the last 8 years I worked as Photo Editor of the biggest news website in Brazil, UOL. There I led a team of freelance photographers, made the visual edition of special reportages and managed big events photo coverages.

I've built my career with all care and love that you can see in those photos. Mainly focused on editorial market, my path walks side by side with most important publications in Brazil, like Folha de S. Paulo, Editora Abril, Editora Globo, Trip, and others.

Here I'll keep sharing a bit of this story.

Living in Europe since early 2023. Lisbon based, open to the world.
