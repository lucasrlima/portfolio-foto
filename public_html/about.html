Overview About Photos


Lisbon based, I was born and raised in São Paulo, Brazil, and have worked across photography and journalism for over 20 years, with a current focus on portraiture and Cultural Heritage.

I worked as a Photo Editor at UOL, one of Brazil’s largest news platforms, where I led a network of freelance photographers, edited visual content for in-depth reports, and managed photographic coverage of major events.

My career has been shaped within the editorial field, collaborating with some of Brazil’s most important publications, including Folha de S. Paulo, Editora Abril, Editora Globo, and Editora Trip.
